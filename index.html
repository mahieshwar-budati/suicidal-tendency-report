A Suicidal Ideation Detection Framework on Social Media Using Machine Learning and Genetic Algorithms

ABOUT

In today’s modern world peoples are facing lots of competition in all fields such as Professional working environment, education environment, sports and many more. This growing competition leading to stress and this stress may develop suicidal tendency which has to be detected on time to prevent peoples from taking such suicidal step.

Whenever person is in deep stress then often will stress will exhibit in his tweets/posts which he spread in social media and by analysing such POSTS we can detect depression and take necessary steps to help person from handling stress.

Cleaned text will be further process to generate Original and Linguistic Features where Original Features contains POS (part of speech extraction from sentence), NGRAM, Topics word extraction and Statistic Features extraction (statistic features means Number of words, characters, total sentences and total paragraphs) and then TFIDF (convert text into numeric values) features extraction.
